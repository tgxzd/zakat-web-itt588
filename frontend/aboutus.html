History of Zakat Murni

Zakat Murni was established in 2024 by Tengku Aizad and a group of compassionate individuals who aimed to make a difference in the lives of those in need. Driven by the core Islamic principle of Zakat, our organization strives to alleviate poverty and uplift communities.
